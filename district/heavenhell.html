Heaven&Hell is a district separate from all others, under different rules.
This is a directory of Fediverse instances that are friendly (or hostile) towards the idea of users posting mutual aid posts.

We hope that this resource will help you choose a new home at Fediverse, keeping in mind the safety and livelihood of those in poverty. Anyone can submit an instance, unlike on regular districts where you need to be staff or have their permission to add an instance to feDistricts.

Your instance must be open for new users (no friends-only communities)
